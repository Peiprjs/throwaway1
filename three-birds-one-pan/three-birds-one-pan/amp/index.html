Three birds, one pan

So there I was, browsing r/egg_irl like the egg I am, and stumbled across this Tumblr post. And as such, I put my brain to work.

It’s not going to be two, it’s going to be three: boymode, girlmode and andromode. This could make it or break it, IDK. The story is going to be written by an internal narrator, from the point of view of Katie Claudsson, the female main character. It’s going to be roughly equiparable to real time, in an unimportant location.

I expect the story to have two main seasons: “Birds of a feather” and “Birds of a nest”. You can guess how they go.
